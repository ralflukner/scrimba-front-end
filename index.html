Dr. Ralf Lukner MD PhD - Board Certified in Internal Medicine

Dr. Lukner is Board Certified in Internal Medicine. Internal Medicine is the medical specialty dealing with the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of a broad range of diseases. As a General Internist, Dr. Lukner is uniquely qualified to practice primary care and follow patients through their adult lives on an outpatient at the clinic.

Dr. Lukner provides the following types of medical care:

  • Precise diagnoses of and prompt treatment for a broad range of symptoms and diseases
  • Medical advice in conditions that affect any of the body's systems
  • Guidance, counseling, and preventive interventions for improved overall health
  • Life-long comprehensive care and palliative care
  • Care for mental health disorders, such as depression and anxiety caused by abnormal activities in the brain, a chronic disease, or hormonal imbalances
