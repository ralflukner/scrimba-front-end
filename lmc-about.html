I am a primary care physician who see adult outpatients at my clinic, Lukner Medical Clinic, LLC, in Pampa, TX.  I diagnose and treat a variety of conditions, from infections to strokes, cardiac, cancer, and kidney diseases. I take nearly all insurances. Televisit Appointments over the web or phone are available.   Please call our office for details.
